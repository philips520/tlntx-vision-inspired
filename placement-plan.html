Placement plan

A simple plan that keeps the process from getting sloppy.

We usually work in phases: audit, fix, rehearse, apply smarter, then tighten based on feedback from the market.

Phase 1 — Audit

Target role review, market fit, gaps, and where your current profile is underselling you.

Phase 2 — Rebuild

Resume edits, profile framing, credibility markers, and communication cleanup.

Phase 3 — Rehearse

Mock rounds, answer structure, confidence work, and common weak points.

Phase 4 — Execute

Applications, outreach, tracking, and process discipline that does not fall apart after week one.
